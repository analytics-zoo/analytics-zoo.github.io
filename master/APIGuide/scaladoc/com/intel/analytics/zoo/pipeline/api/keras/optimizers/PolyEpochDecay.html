PolyEpochDecay 

case class PolyEpochDecay(power: Double, maxEpochs: Int) extends LearningRateSchedule with Product with Serializable

A learning rate decay policy, where the effective learning rate follows a polynomial decay, to be zero by the max_epochs. Calculation: init_lr * (1 - epoch/max_iteration) ^ (power)

power

The coefficient of decay.

maxEpochs

The maximum number of epochs when lr becomes zero.
